how does that text editor work?

basically its ripped off from showdown's example page, which is the js library that I'm using to render the markdown. there's two objects, one of them a textarea, and the other a span. when you connect to the page, it sets up a websocket connection to a group specific to that sample, and then it grabs the current state of the entry from its peers. then, as the page changes, showdown grabs the value of the textarea and renders it. that's it!
